1A. Project Brief

Laika is a highly decorative display typeface that presents a marriage of a continuous staircase form and exaggerated curves.

1B. Intro

The name Laika belongs to a Soviet dog who became one of the first animals to orbit the earth. Inspired by the idea of space and the unknown, Laika is a transformative typeface. It contains high contrast strokes that combine grids and smooth rounded edges into letterforms, and it is best to serve for display and advertising purposes.

5. Reflection

Through this project, I've gotten the chance to experience what it's like to design a typeface. From researching what the typeface would look like, to sketching out letterforms and countless times of editing and refining on glyphs, I've realized that designing a typeface comes with many challenges.
The one thing that I really struggled with is maintaining consistency in my letterforms. I've noticed that deciding what my design features are is very significant in the design process. Otherwise, my letters would never look unified. Since I'm not personally satisfied with how some of the letters look, I'm hoping to make this an ongoing project on exploring customized letterforms.
