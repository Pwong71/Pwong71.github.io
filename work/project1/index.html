ABOUT
The term ‘Sik Fan’ originally means eat rice in Cantonese. But in my family, this term can represent so much more. When my mom yells ‘SIK FAN(食飯)!!’ from the kitchen, that means delicious food is all ready on the dinner table. The term also suggests family time, time to get together and catch up on what happened during the day while we enjoy some exquisite home cooking. Opening Sik Fan has always been my mom’s dream, she pours her entire soul into making her family well-fed and satisfied. To the Wongs, my mom’s cooking is where home is. And now we’re ready to show the world what home means to us, 食飯喇!
