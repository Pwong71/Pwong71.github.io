1B. Intro

“Lost in Space” is an editorial that addresses the lack of mental support within the LGBTQ2+ community. The goal of this project is to research a target market and produce visual communication material tailored for that market.

2A. Challenge

To create two magazine spreads with an infographic and an art direction that appeals to the target demographic.
