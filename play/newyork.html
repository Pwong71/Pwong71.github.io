Travelling to New York for a week right before Christmas was hands down the most magical feeling I’ve ever had. Taking photos during this trip reminded me of my 15 year old self that had just moved to Vancouver. I would go out almost every weekend just to explore new places and shoot purely for the sake of documenting beautiful moments in my life and leaving footprints on this world. Five years later, I find myself in New York feeling nostalgic as I capture the hustle and bustle of this sleepless city.

Travelling itself is full of bittersweetness: on one hand, you don’t want the trip to end, but on the other, it is a precious gift to have the opportunity to capture fleeting moments and be able to look back on them later.
